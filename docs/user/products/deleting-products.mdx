---
title: "Delete a Product"
description: "How to Delete a Product in the Spree Commerce open-source eCommerce platform"
---

To delete a product, find the product you’d like to delete in the Products tab of the admin dashboard and click on it.<img alt="Find The Product" classname="block dark:hidden" src="https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1733761978/docs/Products/Delete%20a%20Product/2.1.2.1_Deleting_Products_-_Find_The_Product_y2mjgn.png" />Doing so will open the product editor for that particular product.<img alt="Edit a Product" classname="block dark:hidden" src="https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1733761979/docs/Products/Delete%20a%20Product/2.1.2.2_Deleting_Products_-_Product_Editor_gmjnrd.png" />Scroll down to the bottom of the page and locate the red Delete button in the bottom right corner and click on it.

A window popup will ask you to confirm this action. Press OK and the product will be deleted.<img alt="Click Delete And Confirm" classname="block dark:hidden" src="https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1733761979/docs/Products/Delete%20a%20Product/2.1.2.3_Deleting_Products_-_Click_Delete_And_Confirm_zhey82.png" />The product will now be removed from your store.<img alt="Product Removed" classname="block dark:hidden" src="https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1733761978/docs/Products/Delete%20a%20Product/2.1.2.4_Deleting_Products_-_Product_Removed_kpzd5j.png" />Note: If a product is linked to an order, then you cannot delete it. Instead, set it’s Status to Archived.
